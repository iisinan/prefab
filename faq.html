Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our operations and services.

What areas do you cover in Nigeria?

Currently, we have major operations in Kaduna and Abuja, but our consultancy and engineering services are available nationwide across all 36 states.

How cost-effective are CNG buses?

CNG fuel is significantly cheaper than diesel, offering up to 25-30% savings in operational costs while reducing carbon emissions by over 40%.

Do you support local state governments?

Yes, we specialize in government-level infrastructure partnerships, providing end-to-end management for transit and smart city initiatives.
