Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Read how we protect your data.

Effective: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Prefab Technologies Limited, we collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company affiliation when you submit inquiries or contact forms.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system for security and performance optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Responding to your infrastructure and service inquiries
  • Improving our website experience and service offerings
  • Sending relevant project updates and corporate newsletters (only with your consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations under Nigerian law

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted communications, secure servers, and access-controlled databases. We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable international data protection standards.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Prefab Technologies Limited does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties. Exceptions include trusted partners who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • File a complaint with the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies are used for theme preferences, session management, and anonymous analytics. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.
